Time tracking

WebWork is a cross-platform tracking tool which helps you accurately track your working day by monitoring computer usage. It will take random screenshots, record activity level, active applications and websites. All that data will be available by visual report, which can be easily turned into exportable documents. Tracking your billable and non-billable hours will give you stronger sense how you spend time on each project, contract and task. Which will help you find the optimal conditions to improve productivity and get better results for your projects

Employee monitoring

WebWork Tracker is designed to help you monitor your employees’ productivity by gaining complete transparency of their desktop activities. It will take random screenshots of your employee’s screen, will count mouse clicks and keystrokes, also will give you detailed data about apps and websites your employees use and visit at working time. WebWork is #1 time tracking solution which combines all essential tools for employee monitoring and tracking work time for freelancers, remote workers, employees, team members or even for personal use. Real-time dashboard also will help you always be aware what your employees are doing at that moment.
